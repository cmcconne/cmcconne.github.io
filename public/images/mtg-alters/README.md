# MTG alters & artist proofs

Put alter / artist-proof / signed-card photos here (JPG, portrait, MTG card
proportions ~488×680 or larger), then register each one in
`scripts/mtg-alters.json`. Filenames are arbitrary — the manifest maps them to
cards. They surface on the Magic deck-detail page as an "Alters & proofs"
gallery, a badge in the decklist, and the card-image preview.
